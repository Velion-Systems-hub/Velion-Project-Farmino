Everyone should have access to nutritious food

Access to nutritious food is a basic human right, yet millions still face hunger and malnutrition. Despite progress in agriculture and distribution, poverty, inequality, and poor infrastructure keep many communities from eating well..

Nutritious food is key to health and learning. Supporting efforts like community farms and school meals helps end hunger and build a healthier future.
